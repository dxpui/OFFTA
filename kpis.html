Key performance indicators

Throughput KPI

The Throughput KPI is indicative of the industrial strength of LLU processes and measures the actual lines delivered. The figures quoted exclude BT downstream connections.

Last updated: 12 April 2024

Source: 12 April 2024

LLU Provision KPI

Local Loop Unbundling (SMPF / MPF) Provision. The figures quoted exclude BT downstream connections.

Last updated: 9 May 2024

Source: Openreach

LLU Repair KPI

Local Loop Unbundling (SMPF / MPF) Repair. The figures quoted exclude BT downstream connections.

Last updated: 9 May 2024

Source: Openreach

Wholesale Line Rental KPI

The Wholesale Line Rental (WLR) KPI measures the Openreach performance in the Delivery of WLR orders i.e. delivered on the agreed ‘customer committed date’ and ‘working’.

Last updated: 9 May 2024

Source: Openreach
